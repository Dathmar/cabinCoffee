About Cabin-Coffee

When we think origins of these coffee shops, often our minds go to privileged dudes who were inspired by some stuff they saw in Italy or well-off corporate execs who were inspired by a moose they saw on some Alaskan ski trip. But when I think coffee, I think of that warm cup that kept us all going when times were hard and we were struggling. I'm talking about the real dudes who were up at 5:30 in the morning to catch that first train out every morning just to make that paycheck - Cranking out those 12-hour days traveling across several towns just trying to make it in life.
Fortunately, when you're making that walk to the train station or bus stop having that first cigarette, some of those local cafés were open at the crack of dawn - and you had a chance to warm up with a cup of coffee and experience at least one good thing in life. It was a chance to briefly rest and enjoy some tranquility and remind yourself not to give up. And to keep going. You don't know where life will take you next year or even tomorrow.

But you keep going. You don’t know where you’ll be tomorrow but that never stopped you before.
