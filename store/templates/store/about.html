About Cabin-Coffee

I’d like to thank you for visiting our web page. I built this little coffee shop for many reasons. First, my favorite chain had left the state of Illinois. I spent many days in their shops both visiting and working. Many years after, I wanted to see another cabin-themed coffee shop come to the Chicagoland area.

During my time there, I would bring home the espresso blend to my mother. She would grind it and brew it fresh for us every morning. That was our way of bonding as a family. Today, it is that memorable taste I sought to share through my own coffee shop. The Cabin-Coffee blend is a medium-dark roast made for espresso or cold brew. It also makes a smooth drip coffee sporting a very balanced flavor with prominent notes of chocolate. It is neither citrusy nor chalky which are the flavors I have always avoided. The beans are roasted by our good friend over in Roselle, a town I loved growing up.
Our Cold Brew is steeped for 24 hours and served with your choice of flavored syrups and a variety of dairy or non-dairy options. And if you’re like me and trying to stay lean, I can give you a shot of cold espresso in lieu of the sugary syrups. I want people to walk away impressed knowing they got the cold coffee they wanted. You can find my locations on the main page of this site where I’ll be listing upcoming events.

On a final note, I’d like to mention that ten percent of my earnings will be used to help restore and maintain the historical Zion Church in Carpentersville. I have been blessed in more ways than I can imagine, so I’d like to use this enterprise as a means of paying it forward.

Thanks for your visit!
Rich@cabin-coffee.com
